## SECCIÓN 5: CÓMO FUNCIONA (ARQUITECTURA SIMPLIFICADA)

### Eyebrow:
How it works

### Headline (H2):
**Private AI in Three Steps**

### Layout: Tres pasos visuales con flechas/línea de conexión

---

**Step 1: You Send a Request**
Your data is encrypted before it leaves your device. It travels through a secure channel that no one — including NEAR AI — can intercept.

**Step 2: Processing Inside a Hardware Vault**
Your request enters a Trusted Execution Environment (TEE) — a locked area inside Intel and NVIDIA processors where data stays encrypted even while being processed. The cloud provider, the operating system, and NEAR AI operators have zero access.

**Step 3: You Get Results + Proof**
You receive your AI response along with a cryptographic attestation — a tamper-proof certificate proving your data was processed privately and was never accessed or modified.

## NOTAS DE IMPLEMENTACIÓN

### Principios de copy aplicados:
1. **Beneficio primero, tecnología después** — Cada sección abre con lo que el usuario gana, luego explica cómo.
2. **Jerga eliminada o explicada** — TEE → "hardware vault" / "locked area inside processors". Attestation → "cryptographic proof" / "tamper-proof certificate".
3. **Segmentación de audiencias** — Developers, Enterprises, Government reciben copy y CTAs específicos.
4. **Proof points concretos** — "<100ms", "30 seconds", "99.5%", "$0.15/M" en lugar de "fast", "reliable", "affordable".
5. **CTAs específicos y diferenciados** — "Get API Keys", "Try Private Chat", "Schedule a Demo" en lugar de "Contact Us" genérico.
6. **SEO** — H1 único, meta description con keywords, headings con intent de búsqueda.
7. **Contraste trust vs verify** — Hilo conductor que posiciona a NEAR AI como la alternativa verificable.
